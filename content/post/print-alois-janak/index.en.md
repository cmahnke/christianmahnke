Alois Janák was born in Czechoslovakia in 1924, studied in Prague and fled to Munich after the Second World War. In 1982 he moved to Switzerland.

The picture shows a "Buddelflugzeug" (an aeroplane enclosed in a bottle, like a ship in a bottle). It's dated 1997.
