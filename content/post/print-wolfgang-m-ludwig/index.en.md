Wolfgang M. Ludwig studied Fine Art at the [University of Fine Arts in Braunschweig](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hochschule_f%C3%BCr_Bildende_K%C3%BCnste_Braunschweig). According to the Kunstmarkt catalogue, he was living in [Braunschweig](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Braunschweig) at the time the print was created. The Internet Archive contains an [archived snapshot of him at the Peine District Museum](https://web.archive.org/web/20080413051105/ http://www.kreismuseum-peine.de/ludwig.htm). He also has a [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/w.ludwigart/).

The Göttingen Art Market was an annual event that took place from 1970 to 1987. This [article from the Göttinger Tageblatt](https://www.goettinger-tageblatt.de/lokales/goettingen-lk/goettingen/1970-erfunden-lange-ein-spektakel-MIAN7QKPKXQX6IWS6IG7G67STY.html) provides an overview.
