[Leslie G. Hunt](https://www.leslieghunt.com/) is a contemporary American artist who lives and works in Frankfurt am Main. He is known for his drawings of technical objects, which are often enriched with interesting details.

For example, this image features a reference to [Marbach an der Donau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marbach_an_der_Donau), as well as to the [Danube Cycle Path](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donauradweg) (German), which was being built around the time the etching was created.

The print is dated 1992.
