Max Baur was a German photographer; his work is associated with [New Objectivity](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neue_Sachlichkeit_(Fotografie)) (German). He completed an apprenticeship as a photographer in Eisenach from 1916 to 1919 and became particularly well known for his precise architectural and advertising photographs.

The image is a [silver gelatine print](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gelatin_silver_print) and depicts cherry blossoms, presumably from a [Prunus cerasus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prunus_cerasus).
