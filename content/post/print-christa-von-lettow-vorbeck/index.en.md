Not much can be found out about Christa von Lettow-Vorbeck, except that she was presumably the sister of the notorious General [Paul von Lettow-Vorbeck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_von_Lettow-Vorbeck).

Gerbrand Caspers has published an extensive post about [Lettow-Vorbeck](https://gerrie-thefriendlyghost.blogspot.com/2013/04/christa-von-lettow-vorbeck-berlin.html) on his blog ["The Linosaurus"](https://gerrie-thefriendlyghost.blogspot.com/), in which this motif can also be found.

What is interesting about this print is the signature: the surname is given only as "v. Lettow".
