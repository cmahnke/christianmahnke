Holzschnitt von Christa von Lettow-Vorbeck (1881-1945)

Friday, November 19, 2021
Updated on
Wednesday, April 29, 2026

I bought a woodcut by Christa von Lettow-Vorbeck from Zeitzeug…

Print
Signature

Not much can be found out about Christa von Lettow-Vorbeck, except that she was presumably the sister of the notorious General Paul von Lettow-Vorbeck.

Gerbrand Caspers has published an extensive post about Lettow-Vorbeck on his blog “The Linosaurus”, in which this motif can also be found.

What is interesting about this print is the signature: the surname is given only as “v. Lettow”.

Update 29 April 2026

  • Gerbrand Caspers has also dedicated a post to Christa von Lettow-Vorbeck in “DAS HAUS DER FRAU” – the print shown here can also be found there.
  • The Archivportal-D contains some archival material on her from the Archive of the Academy of Arts.
