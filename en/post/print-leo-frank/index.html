Colour woodcut from Leo Frank (1884-1959)

Saturday, March 29, 2025

I found a woodcut by Leo Frank (1884-1959) (at artnet)…

Druck
Signatur

The print can also be found in the British Museum collection, (illustration at the Contemporary Art Society). Copies can be found online at various auction houses, the title could be “Birds of Prey in the Highlands” or “Buzzard in the Mist”. On the page of Wolfgang Barina the title is only “Bussarde”. Leo Frank was a Viennese artist who also taught printing and drawing at the Academy of Fine Arts Vienna.
