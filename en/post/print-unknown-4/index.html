Woodcut print of unknown origin

Friday, June 5, 2026

I’ve found a colour woodcut by an unknown artist…

Print

The picture shows either the ruins of a castle or a castle perched on a hill overlooking a wide bend in the river. At the foot of the hill lies a small village by the river. In the foreground are a few conifers.

Although one might think that a relatively large castle overlooking a bend in the river would not be all that common, unfortunately no information has yet been found.
