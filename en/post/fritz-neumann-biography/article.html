New biography of Franz Robert Fritz Neumann (1897-1984) aka Ric available

After the identity of Fritz Neumann and thus Ric could be clarified in October1, Mark Strong from Meibohm Fine Arts2 compiled, condensed and published on AskArt3 on the basis of the findings from my blog4.

Without a paid membership, the full biography can be viewed every Friday, otherwise it is also available for free at Meibohm Fine Arts5.

Many thanks to Mark for his work!
