Painting by Tadeusz Kaczmarek (1937-2006)

Thursday, January 23, 2025

I have found a painting by Tadeusz Kaczmarek (automatic translation) (1937-2006)…

…and because one wants to be on the beach in summer in the middle of winter, I bought it.

Painting
Signature

Tadeusz Kaczmarek was a Polish artist who worked as a painter, graphic artist and interior designer. He lived and worked mainly in the Lower Silesian Voivodeship.

The painting shows the beach of Boltenhagen, Schleswig-Holstein on the Baltic Sea coast.

There is also a blog with some of his works. A very similar painting is also listed there.
