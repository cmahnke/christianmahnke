Projektemacher 2024

A year in review
Monday, December 30, 2024
Christian Mahnke0009-0003-1361-4159
URL: https://christianmahnke.de/en/post/projektemacher-2024/
Keywords:
  • Blog
  • Projektemacher.org

At the end of 2024, here is the annual retrospect for Projektemacher.org. Over 270 articles were published over the course of the year (the most active year to date).

Some of the highlights are

  • 70s wallpaper1 - which was then also published as first HDR IIIF image2.
    • And the corresponding Lightning Talk3 at the IIIF Online Meeting 2024
  • Fritz Neumann (1928-2014) is not Ric!4 - But the right Fritz Neumann (1897-1984) was finally found5
  • On the ruthless nature of metropolitan day-trippers6 - the first entry with text to speech
  • die neue linie - March 19307 - The Bauhaus lifestyle magazine.
  • “Reihenhaus-Fassaden Geschäfts- und Wohnhäuser aus alter und neuer Zeit”8 - Many examples of avant-garde architecture from 1929
  • Licht 20249 - My projects on light and images, like:
    • Contrast enhancement for UV images using HDR10
    • Ultraviolet Photogrammetry11

New pages

  • Vintage Reality12 - Historical stereo images

New functions

  • Clothes hangers13 are now displayed on a map
  • Children’s books14 can now be exported as PDFs
  • Ric - An unknown artist15 is now available in a German translation
  • Web archiving - Some blogs now send their links to the Internet Archive. The blogs themselves will be archived at the DNB in the future.
  • IIIF collections for blog content ( View in Theseus16)
  • 911TV17 is now available18 as a desktop application, e.g. for a physical installation.

Events

  • After my Christmas cards have been very popular in recent years, there was a craft workshop19 this year.
