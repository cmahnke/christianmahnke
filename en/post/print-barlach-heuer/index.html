Colour wood cut by Barlach Heuer (1930-2019)

Friday, September 9, 2022

I have purchased a colour woodcut by Barlach Heuer (French) (1930–2019) (at artnet)…

Sheet
Signature

Barlach Heuer was a painter, graphic artist and glass collector from Elmshorn. He spent many years of his life in Paris, where he also died. The first name “Barlach” probably stems from a family connection to the sculptor Ernst Barlach.

The Elmshorn Art Association dedicated a commemorative exhibition to him in 2020 (German).

The title of the print is “Ygdrasil”, it is a colour woodcut made from two blocks and dates from 1971. It is listed in “Farbdrucke” by the artist, published in 1980 by Nacke in Wuppertal (ISBN: 3-920365-01-1).
