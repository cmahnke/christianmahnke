I have found a woodcut by Otto Richard Bossert (1874-1919)…

Otto Richard Bossert was mainly a graphic artist and illustrator who worked in Leipzig. The SLUB Dresden has the catalogue raisonné digitised

The name of the motif is “Girl looking up from reading”, it is the design for a postcard from 1914.
