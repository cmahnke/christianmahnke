"Liebespaar" (Lovers) by Max Westhäuser (1885-1956)

Tuesday, July 16, 2024

I have acquired a colour woodcut by Max Westhäuser (1885-1956)…

Max Westhäuser was a painter and graphic artist, a representative of the Westphalian Modernism and was a member of the artists’ group “Rote Erde”. The Museum Peter August Böckstiegel is also showing an exhibition about the group in 2023 and an exhibition about Westhäuser in 2015…
