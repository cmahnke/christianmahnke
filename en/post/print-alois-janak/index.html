I bought a colour etching by Alois Janák (born 1924)…

Signature

Alois Janák was born in Czechoslovakia in 1924, studied in Prague and fled to Munich after the Second World War. In 1982 he moved to Switzerland.

The picture shows a “Buddelflugzeug” (an aeroplane enclosed in a bottle, like a ship in a bottle).
