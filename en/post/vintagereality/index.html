VintageReality launched

Tuesday, March 5, 2024

The latest blog incubated at the Projektemacher Labs has been started.

VintageReality will present old stereoscopic images in various display modes in the future.

Presentation

To enable presentations with different glasses and techniques, additional formats and representations are available in addition to the IIIF presentation:

Examples

Anaglyph
Depth map
Wigglegram
  • Anaglyph
  • Depth map
  • Wiggle image, eng. Wigglegram (Both images as animated gif with quick change)

The site also offers additional derivatives:

  • MPO.
  • Full screen - for viewing with a Cardboard VR (or similar) viewer.

Implementation

The various derivatives were partly created with the help of StereoscoPy, OpenCV was used to calculate depth information.
