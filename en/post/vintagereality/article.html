VintageReality launched

The collection of old 3D images now has its own blog
Tuesday, March 5, 2024
Christian Mahnke0009-0003-1361-4159

The latest blog incubated at the Projektemacher Labs1 has been started.

VintageReality2 will present old stereoscopic images in various display modes in the future.

Presentation

To enable presentations with different glasses and techniques, additional formats and representations are available in addition to the IIIF presentation:

Examples

Anaglyph
Depth map
Wigglegram
  • Anaglyph3
  • Depth map4
  • Wiggle image, eng. Wigglegram5 (Both images as animated gif with quick change)

The site also offers additional derivatives:

  • MPO6.
  • Full screen - for viewing with a Cardboard VR7 (or similar) viewer.

Implementation

The various derivatives were partly created with the help of StereoscoPy8, OpenCV9 was used to calculate depth information.
